.. py:function:: count_words(input_file)

   Count unique words in a string.
   Words are made lowercase and punctuation is removed before counting.

   :param input_file: Path to text file.
   :type input_file: str

   :returns: dict-like objedct wheres keys are words and values are counts.
   :rtype: collections.Counter

   .. rubric:: Examples

   >>> count_words("text.txt")
